# AI Job Market Analysis — SQL

## Overview
SQL-based analysis of the global AI job market, exploring demand, salaries, 
and top roles across countries.

## Key Questions Answered
- Which countries have the highest AI job demand?
- What are the top-paying AI roles?
- How is the market distributed across job types?
